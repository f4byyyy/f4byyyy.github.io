Photography in Arc Raiders

Published: 10.03.2026
Arc RaidersGuide

Arc Raiders Settings

For ingame photography it's best to use the binoculars. By pressing F10 the HUD can be hidden. Moreover in the settings under Graphics - Quality set Post-Processing to Medium. This will disable the noise when using the binoculars.

More Tips

  • When you record a video you can capture action much better than with only screenshots. Later you can grab a frame from the video e.g. by using Davinci Resolve.
  • Taking 4k screenshots on a FHD screen (with an NVIDIA GPU): Open the NVIDIA Control Panel and go to "Manage 3D Settings" and set "DSR - Factors" to 4.00x. Now set your screen resolution to 4k in the Windows settings (and enable a higher refresh rate if you want). DSR stands for Dynamic Super Resolution and renders everything in a higher resolution which is then downscaled to your monitors resolution.
  • You can use Light Sticks (red, yellow, green and blue) to create your own lighting and smoke grenades for atmosphere.
